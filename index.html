Giving Harmony

Playing notes of contribution

Giving Harmony is a non-profit organization founded by a group of young musicians with a heart for music and a voice raising awareness in their community. In establishing a sense of harmony, these musicians work to donate to different organizations each year, through concerts and other events. All proceeds are donated to help those in need.
