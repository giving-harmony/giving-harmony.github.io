Giving Harmony

Playing notes of contribution

Giving Harmony Foundation is a non-profit organization. We donate all the proceeds of our concerts to help people in need.

Giving Harmony Audition Application

Hello musicians and parents! Our organization Giving Harmony is looking for aspiring high school musicians and parents to join our non-profit to help us extend a helping hand with each note played! The audition date will be on July 17th, 2022. The deadline will be July 10th. Check out the application form.

Audition Requirements
  • Must be an incoming high school student (9th - 12th grade)
  • 2 contrasting pieces for EACH instrument (i.e. 2 pieces from different time periods, a lyrical & technical piece)
  • No piano accompanist required
  • Bring a copy of the music for the judges
  • Parents are strongly recommended to come to the audition
  • We work as a big family to help those in need, so therefore parents are required to participate in the audition as parents will be helping out the organization as well
  • Be prepared for a short interview session during your audition with you and your parents!
