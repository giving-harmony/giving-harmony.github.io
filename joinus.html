Giving Harmony Audition Application

Hello musicians and parents! Giving Harmony is looking for aspiring young musicians and parents to join our non-profit to help us extend a helping hand with each note played! The audition date will be on May 21, 2023. The deadline will be May 14, 2023. Check out the application form.

Audition Requirements
  • Must be an incoming student of 8th - 12th grade
  • Two contrasting pieces for EACH instrument (two pieces from different time periods, e.g. a piece to demo your technical skills, and a lyrical piece or the piece you may play at our next concert)
  • All solo pieces must be memorized; for ensembles memorization is not required
  • No piano accompanist required
  • Bring a copy of the music for the judges
  • We work as a big family to help those in need, therefore parents are required to participate in the audition as parents will be helping out the organization as well
  • Be prepared for a short interview session during your audition with you and your parents!
